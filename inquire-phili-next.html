About The Event

EnigmaCG’s series of the Analytics Leaders Summit (ALS) having been successfully organised in four different locations- Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia and Thailand in the last two years and attracted over 1000 Data Professionals is now coming to Manila, Philippines with its 7th Edition scheduled to be held from 27-28 June, 2018.

The Philippines being the next big hub for Data Analytics in the region with so many companies especially banks and telco’s turning to analytics is on the verge of a big data boom. The Summit through its speakers will deliver the highest quality content, address the most

current topics related to Predictive Analytics, Big Data, Customer Experience, Loyalty, Talent Shortage and more. With over twenty speakers from across Asia taking the stage showcasing their ideas and presenting case studies on how they have adopted analytics in their organisations, this is an event you cannot afford to miss.
