About Analytics Leaders’ Summit!

Analytics Leaders’ Summit is a series of events exclusively tailored to address the most important challenges analytics professionals are facing today. It is the avenue where real-life case studies from some of the most renowned brands are put together.

We understand the importance of putting things into practice. Thus, Analytics Leaders’ Summit aims to aid you by putting together the most comprehensive learning and networking opportunity through embedded series of conferences and workshops allowing you to get first-hands information on how some of the most prominent organizations in the world leverage the power of data analytics to enhance customers’ journey and reduce business risks.

Who Should Attend?

Learn how to put together an impactful and efficient analytics team while optimizing your available analytics tools. Construct effective data strategy from grounds up to stay ahead of competition and benchmark your data initiatives against your competitors.

If your job scope revolves around the following, then Analytics Leaders’ Summit is a MUST ATTEND event for you:

Marketing Analytics, Risk Analytics, Data Quality, Predictive Analytics, Data Architecture and Engineering, Machine Learning and algorithm, Unstructured Data, Data and Text Mining, Deep Learning, Meta Data and Modeling, Credit Risk, Loyalty/CVM/CLM/CRM, Data Analytics, Big Data Consultants, Customer Insight, Decision Science, Data Science.
